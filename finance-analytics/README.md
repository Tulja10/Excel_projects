# Finance Analytics – AtliQ Hardwares

## Overview

This project analyzes the financial performance of AtliQ Hardwares using Microsoft Excel.
The reports focus on understanding revenue growth, cost trends, and profitability across different years, markets, and regions.
The analysis helps evaluate how sales, costs, and gross margins have changed over time.


## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is provided as part of an Excel training course and represents the financial data of AtliQ Hardwares in the course.
It includes information on net sales, cost of goods sold (COGS), gross margin, and market-level financial performance.
The data is used only for **learning and portfolio demonstration purposes**.


## Reports Included

### 1. P & L by Fiscal Years

This report shows the overall profit and loss performance from **2019 to 2021**.
It highlights strong revenue growth over the years while also showing a slight decline in gross margin percentage.

### 2. P & L by Fiscal Months

This report analyzes monthly sales, costs, and margins.
It helps identify seasonal trends and variations in sales performance throughout the fiscal year.

### 3. P & L by Markets

This report compares financial performance across different markets.
It highlights the markets contributing the most to revenue and profitability.

### 4. GM% by Quarters (Region Analysis)

This report shows gross margin percentages across regions and quarters.
It helps evaluate how profitability varies across different geographical areas.
