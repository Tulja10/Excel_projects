# Sales Analytics – AtliQ Hardwares

## Overview

This project analyzes the sales performance of AtliQ Hardwares using Microsoft Excel.
The goal is to convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that help understand customer performance, market trends, and product growth.


## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is provided as part of an Excel training course and represents the sales data of Atliq Hardwares from the course.
It contains information about customers, markets, products, and yearly sales values.
The data is used only for **learning and portfolio demonstration purposes**.


## Reports Included

### 1. Customer Net Sales Performance

Analyzes sales generated from different customers across 2019–2021.
It highlights revenue growth trends and identifies key customers contributing to total sales.

### 2. Market Performance vs Target

Compares actual sales with target values across different countries.
This report helps identify markets that are underperforming and need attention.

### 3. Top 10 Products

Highlights the best-performing products based on sales revenue.
It also shows year-over-year growth to understand which products drive business performance.

### 4. New Products – 2021

Analyzes products launched in 2021 and their contribution to total sales.
This helps evaluate the success of newly introduced products.
