# Vrinda Store Sales Analysis – 2022

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard analyzing the annual sales performance of **Vrinda Store**, a fictional clothing business.

The goal is to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and channel performance to support better decision-making and improve sales in 2023.


## Dataset

The dataset represents sales transactions from **Vrinda Store in 2022** 
The dataset is used for **learning and portfolio demonstration purposes**.


## Dashboard Analysis

The dashboard combines multiple analyses into a single view:

* **Orders vs Sales Trend** – Monthly trend of orders and revenue to identify seasonality
* **Sales by Gender** – Distribution of sales between male and female customers
* **Order Status** – Breakdown of delivered, returned, cancelled, and refunded orders
* **Top Performing States** – States contributing the most to sales
* **Age Group vs Gender** – Customer segmentation by age and gender
* **Sales Channel Contribution** – Performance of different sales platforms


## Key Insights

* Female customers contribute the majority of total sales
* Most orders come from the **adult age group**
* A few states generate a large share of total revenue
* **Amazon and Myntra** are the top-performing sales channels
* Order delivery rate is high with minimal cancellations and returns
